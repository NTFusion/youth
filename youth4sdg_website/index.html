About Youth Advocate &
Ambassador Program

This program aims to spread SDGs knowledge and promote SDGs actions by recruiting and supporting youth to learn and practice SDGs. Young people who are interested in the SDGs and willing to promote SDGs among their communities will be recruited as Youth4SDG Advocates. Advocates will join in a series of SDGs actions and activities including promoting MY World 2030 Survey. Among the Advocates, those who launch their own SDGs actions, mobilize a fairly large number of young people to join the campaigns, well promote MY World 2030 Survey and other initiatives related to the SDGs and demonstrate strong leadership in the process will be qualified for the title of Youth4SDG Ambassador.

About Youth4SDG Hub Program

Youth4SDG Hub is a global network of youth groups and organizations that are interested in learning, advocating and acting for the Sustainable Development Goals. To become a Youth4SDG Hub partner, an organization will not only join this global network to share and work with others, but also be supported by Youth4SDG secretariat through a various of activities, information and training opportunities.
